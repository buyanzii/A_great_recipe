Simple Omelette Recipe

Delicious fluffy omelette, perfect for a quick meal. This recipe is simple, customizable, and ready in minutes.

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes
  • Serving: 1-2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Your choice of fillings (cheese, veggies, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter/oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and cook until edges are firm.
  4. Add fillings of choice and fold the omelette. Serve hot.
